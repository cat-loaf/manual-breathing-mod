# Manual Breathing <img src="./Content/Items/Accessories/IronLung.png">
You are now manually breathing!

Ever wanted to feel the thrill of manually managing your breath in Terraria? No? Well, too bad, because now you can! This mod adds a quirky little mechanic where you have to keep an eye on your breath, or else... well, you know what happens.

## What's in the Mod?

- **Iron Lung Accessory** <img src="./Content/Items/Accessories/IronLung.png" height="20px">: Because breathing is hard, and you need all the help you can get.
- **Breath UI** <img src="./Content/UI/breath_full.png" height="20px"> <img src="./Content/UI/breath_empty.png" height="20px">: A shiny new UI to remind you that oxygen is important.
- **Iron Lung Guide Dialogue** <img src="https://cdn2.steamgriddb.com/icon/83a368f54768f506b833130584455df4/32/768x768.png" height="20px">: Your friendly NPC guide who may or may not judge your breathing skills.
